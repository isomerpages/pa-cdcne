<p><strong>This November, ArtsEverywhere @ CDC in the North East is where the magic happens!</strong>
</p>
<p>Get ready for a month filled with immersive and exciting performances
by talented groups like RAW Moves and Flame of the Forest. Dive into hands-on
fringe activities, unleash your creativity with fun crafts, and experience
a variety of unique artistic expressions. Check out the full event schedule
and join us for a month of unforgettable moments!</p>
