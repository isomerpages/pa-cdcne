<p>Every Singaporean household will receive $300 CDC Vouchers in January
2026.</p>
<p>The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2026 (January) is part of the enhancements to
the Assurance Package announced at Budget 2025. The package provides more
immediate support for Singaporean households and helps defray daily expenses.</p>
<p>Starting 2 January 2026, you can visit&nbsp;<a href="https://go.gov.sg/cdcv" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">go.gov.sg/cdcv</a>&nbsp;to claim the $300
CDC vouchers. The process for claiming and spending the vouchers remains
the same.</p>
<p>The CDC Vouchers Scheme 2026 (January) is valid till 31 December 2026.</p>
<p><a href="https://vouchers.cdc.gov.sg/residents/info/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Learn</a>&nbsp;how
you can claim your CDC Vouchers 2026 (January).</p>

participating hawkers, heartland merchants and supermarkets in North East.</p>
